How It Works

Four simple steps — from login to a fully organised inbox in seconds

1
📧
Enter Gmail & App Password

You provide your Gmail address and a Google App Password. Credentials held only in session memory — never written to any file or disk.

2
🔒
Secure IMAP Connection

The app opens an encrypted SSL connection to Gmail via imaplib on port 993, fetches your emails into a temporary in-memory DataFrame.

3
🤖
ML & NLP Pipeline Classifies

A trained ML model with NLP preprocessing — tokenization and TF-IDF — runs entirely on your machine, categorising each email automatically.

4
📊
View Your Smart Dashboard

Results appear instantly on your Streamlit dashboard. Filter by category, scan priorities. Session ends — everything is wiped.

Why MailSenseAI
Is Secure

MailSenseAI uses your Gmail App Password — a special 16-character password from your Google account granting limited, revocable inbox access. Your real password is never entered.

Connection uses Python's imaplib over encrypted SSL/TLS on port 993. Credentials live only in RAM for the active session — never written to any file, database, or disk.

All email classification happens 100% on your own machine. Email content is never sent to any external server, cloud API, or third-party service.

When your session ends, all data is cleared from memory automatically. No database, no logs, no persistent storage of any kind.
